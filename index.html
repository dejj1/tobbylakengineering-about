About Us

Tobbylak Engineering Nig Ltd is an indigenous company formed by a seasoned professional in the year 2006 and fully registered with Corporate Affairs Commission on 13th October, 2006 under the companies and allied matters act 1990. Before this company was incorporated, we have been delivering professional services under the umbrella of our two sister companies, named Tobbylak ventures and Bisol Engineering Works, both registered with Corporate Affairs Commission on the 26th November, 1999 and 12th November, 2004.

Tobbylak Engineering Nigeria Limited has been rendering professional services in the area of building and civil engineering construction, renovation works, facility management for banks and private organizations. We are ready to execute projects of any magnitude by virtue of our wealth of experience and competence.

Should our company be considered for jobs, we promise to give you the best of our quality services as we have done in the recent past.
